## GOALS
- Analyze an attack vector and use the provided information to identify critical assets that can be remediated to reduce the attack surface
---
## TASKS
1. Navigate to `Risks & Vulnerabilities` > `Attack Vector`.
2. In the center of your screen, a potential **Attack Vector** will be shown. An attack vector is a potential pathway to critical assets, and Claroty models how the attacker could potentially move through the enviornment and impact other assets.
    - These potential movements are detailed under **Attack Chain** on the right side of the page.
3. Select **Target Zone**, choose an alternate zone within, and note the new attack vector displayed.
    - Each attack vector shows a diferent potential attack pathway.
    - These attack vectors help visualize vulnerable assets, allowing remediation to be enacted with further context.
4. Right-clicking on one of the attack vector nodes will take you to that asset's page.
